About Learning Schedule

What Is Learning Schedule?

Learning Schedule is an online profile.

It is an online profile that has been designed to allow you to easily track and showcase your learning.

It has been designed to be aesthetic, fast and functional.

Why did I create Learning Schedule?

I wanted to create a platform for self-learners to showcase their learning, knowledge and passion.

The reason is quite simple.

It bothers me that employers will favour someone with a university degree, over someone who is passionate and rediculously knowledgable beyond most, yet did not have the privilege to study at University.

It's utter bullshit.

I aim to dissolve the stigma attached to self-learning, or rather, this idea of institutionalised education is necessarily the best path to becoming a working professional.

I do not believe that you need to find yourself $40,000+ into debt, in order to study and be the best at your craft.

That is why I created Learning Schedule.

How does it work?

Here's the basic gist of it.

The idea is that your Learning Schedule profile appears in Google Search whenever someone searches for your name.

They see what you've learnt. They like what they see.

I think that's pretty cool.

In addition, it keeps you passionate with your self-learning, you pimp out your profile and showcase all your amazing learning achievements.

How was it built?

This application was with utilising React/Redux on the front-end and Node.js on the backend.

Rather than rely on static pages, all profiles are actually single page applications - meaning it's super zippy and seamlessly fluid.

Functionality and features are constantly being added.
